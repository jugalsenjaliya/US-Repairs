About Us

From fridge freezers to smartphones, laptops to smart homes, we're here to help with all life’s kit – whatever it is and wherever you bought it.

We believe everyone has the right to expert help with all technology. That’s why, whatever it is and wherever you bought it, we’ll help you get more from the kit that matters most to you.
When you’re looking for something new, our nationwide team of in-store experts offers friendly, impartial advice – and we’ll help you get it home and set it up with no fuss.
And when something goes wrong – from smashed smartphones to leaking dishwashers – we’ll help put it right. Whether it’s 24/7 tech support over the phone or a home repair visit at a time that’s right for you, you’re in safe hands with us.
Finally, when you need help even faster, we’re there for you online. We’ve got hundreds of Kit Guides for everything from smartphone set-up to PC spring-cleaning – and our Discover blog brings you fresh insights and up-to-date info on the kit that matters to you.
Online, in store or over the phone, we’re here to help with all life’s kit.
