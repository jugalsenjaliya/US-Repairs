MacBook Repairs

If your MacBook’s in a mess, we'll get it sorted. Our Apple-accredited repair experts are standing by to help at your local store.

See our laptop repair experts in action:

  • We’ll give you a free in-store diagnosis – just bring your laptop in and we’ll give you an immediate quote for the repair
  • No fix, no fee – if we can’t repair your laptop, you won’t pay a penny
  • It doesn’t matter where you bought it – we’ll fix it
  • All our repairs are guaranteed for 12 months

We can repair:

  • MacBook Pro 13 inch
  • MacBook Pro M1
  • MacBook Pro 16 inch
  • MacBook Air
  • MacBook Air M1

Terms & Conditions

Computer problems are notoriously hard to diagnose, let alone fix – so why not bring your Mac in-store and let our experts handle the tough stuff?
We’ll run a free in-store diagnostic to figure out exactly what’s wrong with your laptop, then give you a no-obligation quote for the repair.
If it’s a software issue, we’ll fix it in-store – usually the very same day. For hardware problems, we’ll do everything we can to repair it there and then, but if necessary we’ll send it to our National Repair Centre for some expert attention.
Once our repair experts have checked over your laptop, we’ll give you a call to confirm the cost of the replacement parts – and once it’s sorted, you can come back in-store to pick it up at a time that suits you.
