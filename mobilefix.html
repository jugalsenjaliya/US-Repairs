Terms & Conditions

If your mobile’s broken – whether it’s a cracked screen, busted battery or just about anything else – our repair experts can get it sorted fast.
Just bring it into your nearest US Repairs Service Centre and we’ll give you an immediate quote – including all parts and labour, with no hidden charges to sting you down the line. And if the fault’s covered by your warranty, the repair won’t cost you a thing.
If you visit one of our state-of-the-art Repair & Service Centres, we can often repair your phone by the very next day – but if not, we’ll get it back to you within 14 days, and often sooner.
But in any case, we’ll fix it with genuine manufacturer’s parts wherever possible, and test it to their same stringent standards – and for your peace of mind, all our repairs are backed by a full 12-month guarantee.
