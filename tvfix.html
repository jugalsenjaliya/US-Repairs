Terms & Conditions

If your TV’s broken down, you need it fixed with no dramas – and your local US Repairs repair experts are ready and waiting to help.
Just give us a call, and we’ll pull out all the stops to get your telly back up and running, no matter where you bought it originally.
We’ll arrange a visit at a time that suits you, Monday to Saturday, and call to let you know 30 minutes before we arrive. Then our experts will pick your TV up and get straight down to work, so you can get on with your life.
Once we’ve taken a look, we’ll call to confirm the cost of any parts required and, if you agree, carry out the repair.
When we’re done, we’ll drop your kit back home at a time that suits you, with all our work guaranteed for twelve months – and in the unlikely event that we can’t fix it, you won’t pay a single penny.
